VA Streamline Refinance FAQ

The most common questions veterans ask about the VA IRRRL program, answered.

A VA Interest Rate Reduction Refinance Loan (IRRRL) lets veterans with an existing VA loan refinance to a lower rate or switch from adjustable to fixed with minimal paperwork. It typically does not require a new appraisal, income verification, or credit score minimum.

Most VA Streamline Refinances close in 10 to 14 days. Because there is no appraisal and no income documentation, the process moves much faster than a conventional refinance.
