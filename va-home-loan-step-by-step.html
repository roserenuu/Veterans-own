Buying A Home

VA Home Loan Simplified: A Step By Step Guide

A VA loan can feel complicated at first. This guide breaks the process into clear steps so you know what comes next.

Last reviewed June 20266 minute read

Step 1: Check Eligibility

Start by confirming whether your service history may qualify you for VA home loan benefits. Your lender may help retrieve your Certificate of Eligibility.

Step 2: Get Pre Approved

A real pre approval reviews income, credit, debts, assets, and the estimated payment range. This helps you shop with confidence and avoid homes outside your budget.

Step 3: Make An Offer With A VA Ready Team

Work with a real estate agent and lender who understand VA requirements. A strong offer package can help sellers feel comfortable with VA financing.

Step 4: Appraisal, Underwriting, And Conditions

The property needs an appraisal and must meet VA requirements. Underwriting reviews the borrower and the property. You may be asked for updated documents or explanations.

Step 5: Closing

Before closing, you will review final numbers. Make sure you understand the payment, cash to close, funding fee, escrows, and any lender credits or seller credits.

Do not skip: A home inspection is different from a VA appraisal. Even if an inspection is not required for the VA loan, many buyers still choose one to understand the home's condition.
