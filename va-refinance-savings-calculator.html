Good To Know

Payment Is Only One Part Of A Refinance

Costs Matter

Closing costs, escrows, payoff changes, and the VA funding fee can affect the total benefit of refinancing.

Break-Even Matters

If the refinance saves money monthly, compare the savings with costs to understand how long it takes to break even.

Loan Type Matters

An IRRRL may fit an existing VA loan payment goal, while cash-out may fit equity, debt payoff, or non-VA refinance goals.
