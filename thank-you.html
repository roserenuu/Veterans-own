What Happens Next

A Specialist Reviews Your Request

You do not need to submit another form. The team will use what you shared to start the right VA purchase, refinance, cash-out, or credit conversation.

Review

A VA loan specialist reviews your goal, state, credit range, loan type, and contact preferences.

Call

The team reaches out by phone, text, or email. You can call first if you want faster help.

Plan

You get a clear next step: purchase pre-approval, IRRRL quote, cash-out review, or credit readiness plan.

Prepare These If Available

Helpful Documents

You do not need everything before the first conversation, but these can speed up a quote or pre-approval.

  • Most recent pay stubs or income award letters
  • W-2s or tax returns if requested
  • Current mortgage statement for refinance
  • Homeowners insurance and tax estimate
  • Certificate of Eligibility if you have it
  • Bankruptcy discharge or trustee details if applicable
